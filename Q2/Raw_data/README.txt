The data is given by the professor to do the Assignment on the date of Feb 12, 2024.
Description: This dataset contains information on the gender, race/ethnicity, parental level of education, lunch type, test preparation course completion, and scores for math, reading, and writing for 1000 students.
Format: CSV

Fields:

Gender: student's gender (male/female)
Race/Ethnicity: student's race/ethnicity (group A, group B, group C, group D, group E)
Parental Level of Education: highest level of education attained by student's parent(s)/guardian(s) (some high school, high school, some college, associate's degree, bachelor's degree, master's degree, doctoral degree)
Lunch: type of lunch student received (standard, free/reduced)
Test Preparation Course: whether or not the student completed a test preparation course (none, completed)
Math Score: student's score on math section of exam (0-100)
Reading Score: student's score on reading section of exam (0-100)
Writing Score: student's score on writing section of exam (0-100)
Units: None
